Here's what this code does in simple words:

The Goal: Create a visual picture of words where bigger words are the ones that matter most in a text.

Step-by-step breakdown:

1. Import Tools - Brings in libraries for text processing and creating visuals

2. Get Stopwords - Downloads a list of common, boring words (like "the", "is", "and") that don't add real meaning

3. Create Sample Text - Uses a movie review as an example: *"The acting in this movie was phenomenal!..."*

4. Split Into Words - Breaks the review into individual words

5. Remove Common Words - Removes all the stopwords from the review

6. Add Your Own Words to Remove - You can decide extra words to skip (in this case: "movie", "plot", "characters", "ending")

7. Filter Words - Keeps only the important, meaningful words remaining

8. Create Word Cloud - Makes a cool visual display where:
   - Bigger words = appear more often or are important
   - Smaller words = appear less often
   - Random colors = makes it look nice

9. Display It - Shows the colorful word cloud on screen with white background

In summary: It takes text (like a review), removes boring filler words, and creates a beautiful visual where you can quickly see the most important words at a glance!
